About ReadSMART

ReadSMART is a learning companion built to strengthen every student’s reading comprehension through engaging passages, reflection, and practice.

It focuses on three core levels of understanding:

  • Literal — grasping the facts and details
  • Inferential — uncovering hidden meanings and insights
  • Critical — thinking deeply and forming your own judgments

SMART stands for Strategic · Measured · Active · Reflective · Trackable — guiding values that help readers grow with purpose and joy.

Through consistent practice, ReadSMART inspires every learner to read not just with their eyes, but with their mind and heart. ❤️
